CSC 378 Final Project - PAINBALL

project overview

PAINBALL is a top-down 2D shooter game with a scrappy atmosphere. You play as Franklin, whose purpose is to learn to live an exciting, thrilling life. He is an ordinary person in an extraordinary world, and his circumstances give him the opportunity to add colour to his life, leading him to enter a paintball tournament against a diverse cast of opponents.

As Character Designer and Art Lead, I adapted the designs of the cast from a previous assignment and created walk cycles, shooting animations, idle and blocking animations, and more. I also created environmental assets such as tires and grass to add some "juice" to the game!
